About Us

Krispy Kreme is an American doughnut and coffee chain that is known for its signature glazed doughnuts. The company was founded in 1937 and has since grown to become a global brand with over 1,000 stores in more than 30 countries. In addition to its classic glazed doughnuts, Krispy Kreme offers a variety of other doughnut flavors and coffee drinks.
